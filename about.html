About Me

My name is Mario Baroud and I am a pre-service Mathematics and Computing Teacher.

My Background

I am currently studying the Bachelor of Education (Secondary) at ACU, specialising in Mathematics and Computing. At the start of 2023, I was studying a double degree in Software Engineering and Medical Science at UTS. However, after my experience as a tutor, I decided to switch career paths at the end of 2023 and become a Maths teacher! There's nothing more rewarding than seeing a student go from knowing 0% to 100% in a single lesson.

I work as a Learning Support Officer to gain experience in classrooms, and I also run my own maths tutoring business, Baroud Education, to provide high school students with quality help from tutors who aced their maths HSC exams. I ranked First in the State in Maths Standard 2 and First in Sydney Catholic Schools in Software Design and Development 2022.
